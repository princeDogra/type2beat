WELCOME TO Type2Beat

Welcome to Type2Beat - an interactive platform for Australian Senior Citizen below the age of 65 with little or no symptoms of diabetes. We understand, though you have no signs of diabetes but as per the studies more than half of the people get affected by it after the age of 65. So, through this platform we would help you to monitor your daily glucose intake and manage your medical records and help you & your doctor to view your progress.

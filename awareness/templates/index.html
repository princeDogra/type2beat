WELCOME TO Type2Beat

Welcome to Type2Beat - an interactive platform for Australian Senior Citizen below the age of 65 with little or no symptoms of diabetes. We understand, though you have no signs of diabetes but as per the studies more than half of the people get affected by it after the age of 65. So, through this platform we would help you to monitor your daily glucose intake and manage your medical records and help you & your doctor to view your progress. Additionally, we have various exercises and healthy meal plans that would help you to replenish your strength and prevent diabetes.

TYPE 2 DIABETES AND PREVENTION

1 in every 6 Australian senior citizens age above 65 years suffers from diabetes and its rate increases with age. Therefore it's important to monitor one’s glucose level between the age of 55-65 for taking preventive measures during prediabetic phase.

To prevent Type 2 Diabetes one should:
  • Eat healthy food
  • Drink enough water
  • Exercise regularly
  • Avoid fasting

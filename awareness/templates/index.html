TYPE 2 DIABETES AND PREVENTION

1 in every 6 Australian senior citizens age above 65 years suffers from diabetes and its rate increases with age. Therefore it's important to monitor one’s glucose level between the age of 55-65 for taking preventive measures during prediabetic phase.

To prevent Type 2 Diabetes one should:
  • Eat healthy food
  • Drink enough water
  • Exercise regularly
  • Avoid fasting
